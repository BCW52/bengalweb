Lightweight Performance

Gone are the days when older PCs struggled to keep up with modern software. BengalBoot is engineered to bring out the best in even the most humble hardware. Its lightweight architecture, powered by a minimalistic design and optimized kernel, ensures that your system remains responsive and fast, whether you’re multitasking or running resource-intensive applications. Perfect for developers, students, or anyone who wants to breathe new life into their old machines without compromising performance.

Beginner-Friendly

New to Linux? No problem! BengalBoot is designed with beginners in mind. The installation process is as simple as following a guided wizard, and we offer a comprehensive library of tutorials, tips, and FAQs to get you started. From setting up your first terminal commands to understanding how to customize your system, BengalBoot holds your hand every step of the way.

Customization Options

Personalization is at the heart of BengalBoot. We know that every user has their own style, and with BengalBoot’s deep customization options, you can turn your desktop into a unique expression of you. Choose from an endless array of themes, tweak every icon and font, and make your terminal as cool as your imagination allows. Want to change the startup animation or splash screen? Go ahead! With BengalBoot, your creativity has no limits.

Gaming Ready

Optimized for gaming with built-in support for Steam, Lutris, and other gaming tools. BengalBoot isn't just for coders and creators—it's built for gamers too. Out of the box, you get full support for Steam, Lutris, Proton, and a host of other gaming platforms. Play your favorite titles with optimized performance, thanks to cutting-edge driver support for graphics cards from AMD, NVIDIA, and Intel. With BengalBoot, you don’t need to compromise between fun and functionality. From AAA games to indie hits, your system is ready to deliver an immersive gaming experience.

Web Development Ready

For developers, BengalBoot is a powerhouse ready to serve as your primary development environment. It comes pre-configured with Apache, Nginx, PHP, and MySQL, so you can dive straight into coding and deploying web applications without the hassle of installing and configuring packages. Whether you’re building your portfolio, creating a dynamic web app, or deploying an e-commerce platform, BengalBoot has everything you need to turn your coding dreams into reality.

Open Source

BengalBoot is a labor of love built by the community, for the community. It's 100% open-source, which means you can dive into the code, contribute, suggest features, or even build on top of it for your own projects. Transparency is at our core—there are no hidden features or proprietary lock-ins. By choosing BengalBoot, you’re not just using software; you’re becoming part of a global movement that’s dedicated to keeping technology free, accessible, and innovative for everyone.
